Her gaze, a whisper, tender, sweet,

With every glance, hearts miss a beat.

In those eyes, a story softly told,

Of love and grace, both fierce and bold.

In the velvet night, her eyes alight,

Twin stars that pierce the endless night.

Deep within, a universe unfurls,

A dance of dreams, a world of pearls.

Her lips, a bloom, in dawn's soft kiss,

A touch of magic, pure, and bliss.

With every smile, they sing a symphony,

Of joy, of warmth, in harmony.

Her beauty, timeless, pure and true,

Eyes and lips, a masterpiece in view.

A muse, a dream, in light and shade,

In her embrace, all sorrows fade.
